CCMS Managed Services

DBA-level administration, ongoing optimization, and Tier 2/3 support for your Component Content Management System — so your writers focus on writing.

Expert Administration

A CCMS is a database application with XML-aware workflows, access controls, and publishing integrations. It requires dedicated operational expertise — not just an IT generalist.

User Support (Tier 2/3)

When a writer says "I can't check in this file," it's usually a locked resource, a validation error, or a merge conflict. We are the helpdesk that speaks DITA — resolving content-specific issues that generic IT support cannot diagnose.

Performance Tuning

Optimizing XML database indexing, search configurations, and query performance for large repositories (1M+ objects). We profile slow operations, restructure indexes, and implement caching strategies that keep response times under 2 seconds.

Schema & DTD Updates

Deploying new DTDs, specializations, and constraint modules without breaking legacy content. We test schema changes against the full repository, produce migration scripts, and validate backward compatibility before going live.

Operational Services

Ongoing administration tasks that keep your CCMS healthy and your content team productive.

User Onboarding

Workspace setup, role-based permissions, tool preferences, and authoring environment configuration for new hires — writers are productive on day one.

Platform Migration

Moving from CCMS A to CCMS B (e.g., IXIASOFT to Heretto, Vasont to Paligo). We handle data export, schema mapping, metadata preservation, and link integrity verification.

Repository Cleanup

Identifying and archiving orphaned topics, broken conrefs, unused images, and stale branches. We produce a ROT (Redundant, Outdated, Trivial) report with automated remediation scripts.

Access Control & Auditing

Role-based access control, branch policies, and audit trail configuration for regulated environments. Every view, edit, and export is logged for compliance reporting.

Backup & Disaster Recovery

Scheduled backups, restore testing, and DR runbook development. We ensure your content repository has the same RTO/RPO guarantees as your production databases.

Health Monitoring

Proactive monitoring of system health, storage utilization, indexing performance, and queue backlogs. We alert and remediate before your writers notice a slowdown.

Free CCMS Health Check

We'll audit your CCMS configuration — permissions model, indexing strategy, workflow bottlenecks, and orphan content ratio. You'll receive an actionable report with prioritized recommendations.

Platforms We Support

We provide managed services across the major CCMS platforms — not just one vendor. If your team uses it, we administer it.

IXIASOFT CCMS

Full administration for IXIASOFT 4.x and 5.x — including TEXTML Server tuning, collaborative review configuration, output generator management, and custom dashboard development.

Tridion Docs (SDL)

Publication Manager configuration, condition metadata management, translation integration, and performance optimization for Tridion Docs on-premise and cloud deployments.

Heretto (easyDITA)

Cloud-native administration — API integrations, webhook pipelines, custom publishing configurations, and SSO/SCIM user provisioning for Heretto's SaaS platform.

Paligo

Taxonomy and variable management, publishing portal configuration, Salesforce Knowledge integration, and multi-language workflow setup for Paligo's structured authoring environment.

Vasont / Astoria

Legacy platform support, migration planning, repository maintenance, and publishing pipeline management for Vasont and Astoria CMS installations.

Git-Based & Open Source

For teams running DITA on Git (GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket) with DITA-OT CI/CD pipelines — we provide the same managed service discipline: access control, branch strategy, build monitoring, and publishing automation.

Workflow & Configuration Services

A CCMS is only as effective as its workflows. We design and implement the operational logic that governs how content moves from draft to published.

Review & Approval Workflows

We design multi-stage review chains — author → SME review → editorial → approval → publish — with automated notifications, deadlines, and escalation rules. Workflows adapt to your org structure, not the other way around.

Branching & Release Management

Version branching strategies for parallel product releases, patch documentation, and long-lived maintenance branches. We configure branch policies, merge rules, and baseline snapshots that map to your product release cadence.

Publishing Triggers & Automation

Automated publishing on workflow state change — when content reaches "Approved," the pipeline builds and deploys without manual intervention. We wire CCMS events to DITA-OT builds, webhook-triggered deployments, and notification channels.

Upgrades & Integration Management

Keeping your CCMS current and connected to the tools your teams depend on.

Platform Version Upgrades

Major and minor version upgrades — IXIASOFT 5 → 6, Tridion 14 → 15, Heretto API changes. We run the upgrade in a staging environment, regression-test your customizations, validate content integrity, and execute the cutover with rollback procedures in place.

TMS & Localization Connectors

Configuring and maintaining integrations with translation management systems — Trados, memoQ, Phrase, Memsource. We manage the XLIFF round-trip, status callbacks, and automated handoff triggers so localization runs without manual file shuffling.

JIRA, Git & CI/CD Integration

Connecting your CCMS to engineering workflows — JIRA ticket sync, Git-based content versioning, and CI/CD pipeline triggers. We build and maintain the middleware that keeps content operations synchronized with product development.

Engagement Models

Flexible service models that fit your team size, budget, and operational maturity.

Dedicated Admin

A named Extense CCMS administrator embedded in your team — full-time or part-time. They attend your standups, know your content architecture, and respond within your SLA. Ideal for large teams (20+ writers) with complex configurations.

  • ✓ Named resource, consistent context
  • ✓ 2-hour response SLA (business hours)
  • ✓ Monthly reporting & roadmap reviews

Shared Pool

Access to our CCMS engineering team on a monthly retainer with a block of hours. Tickets are triaged by priority and handled by the specialist with the right platform expertise. Ideal for mid-size teams (5–20 writers) with periodic needs.

  • ✓ Platform-certified specialists
  • ✓ 4-hour response SLA (business hours)
  • ✓ Rollover unused hours (up to 20%)

Project-Based

Fixed-scope engagements for upgrades, migrations, workflow redesigns, or performance overhauls. Defined deliverables, timeline, and budget. Ideal for one-time initiatives or annual maintenance windows.

  • ✓ Fixed price, defined scope
  • ✓ Knowledge transfer included
  • ✓ Post-project support period

Knowledge Transfer & Enablement

We don't create dependencies. Every engagement includes documentation and training so your internal team grows more capable, not more reliant on us.

Admin Runbooks

Step-by-step operational runbooks for every recurring task — user provisioning, schema deployment, backup procedures, and incident response. Your team can execute day-to-day operations independently.

CCMS Admin Training

Hands-on training for your designated CCMS administrators — platform-specific configuration, workflow management, publishing pipeline troubleshooting, and performance monitoring. We train to your actual environment, not generic courseware.

Gradual Handoff

Our engagement model supports a planned transition from fully managed to self-sufficient. We start with full administration, progressively hand off tasks as your team gains confidence, and step back to advisory-only when you're ready.
