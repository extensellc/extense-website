About Extense LLC

Content Engineering • Publishing Automation • AI-Ready Content

Minority-Owned • 8(a) Certified • Woman-Owned Small Business

What We Do

We help organizations turn unstructured documentation into structured, metadata-rich content that powers multi-channel publishing and AI retrieval.

DITA Engineering

Topic modeling, information architecture, specialization, metadata enrichment, and controlled vocabulary design. We build the content architecture — not just the content.

CCMS Implementation

Platform-agnostic selection, configuration, and deployment of Heretto, IXIASOFT, Paligo, and Tridion Docs — with hands-on PoC evaluations using your actual content.

Publishing Automation

CI/CD pipelines for documentation: containerized DITA-OT builds, validation gates, multi-format output (HTML5, branded PDF, JSONL), and deployment to staging and production.

AI-Ready Content

Metadata enrichment, section-aware chunking, controlled vocabulary, and chatbot-ready JSONL export — so your content delivers 85%+ retrieval precision in RAG pipelines.

Content Migration

Proven methodology for converting Word, FrameMaker, InDesign, and unstructured XML to typed, metadata-enriched DITA. Batch automation at scale with quality validation.

System Integration & XML Engineering

CCMS-to-TMS connectors, API integrations, XSLT/XQuery transforms, GraphQL endpoints, and data interchange pipelines connecting content systems to enterprise platforms.

The Transformation

What changes when you move from unstructured to structured content.

Before Extense

  • Content locked in Word/FrameMaker silos
  • Copy-paste between 40+ manuals
  • Multi-week publishing cycles
  • No version control or branching
  • Failed compliance audits
  • Content invisible to AI tools and chatbots
→

After Extense

  • Single-source DITA XML in a CCMS
  • 40–60% content reuse across deliverables*
  • Same-day publishing via containerized CI/CD
  • Full version control with branching & baselines
  • Compliance-ready output (FDA, S1000D, 508)
  • Chatbot-ready JSONL from every build

*Ranges reflect industry benchmarks across comparable engagements.

Our Journey

From a specialized XML consulting practice to a full-service content engineering firm.

2012

Extense LLC Founded

Incorporated in August 2012 as a minority-owned XML and publishing consulting firm in Maryland, focused on structured content for technical documentation.

2013

First Federal Contract

Awarded first government contract, providing DITA engineering and publishing automation services to a major federal agency.

2016

SBA 8(a) Certification

Achieved SBA 8(a) certification, opening doors to sole-source federal contracts and expanding the firm's government portfolio.

2018

CCMS Practice Launched

Expanded service offerings to include full CCMS implementation, integration, and ongoing support for enterprise clients.

2020

Fortune 500 Clients

Grew commercial practice to serve Fortune 500 companies in automotive, financial services, and life sciences sectors.

2024

AI-Readiness & Publishing Automation

Launched AI-ready content practice — metadata enrichment, section-aware chunking, and JSONL export for RAG pipelines — alongside containerized DITA-OT publishing with Docker and CI/CD.

Present

Full-Stack Content Engineering

Today, Extense delivers end-to-end content operations — from strategy and IA through DITA engineering, CCMS, AI-ready output, and automated multi-channel publishing — across government, commercial, and non-profit sectors.
