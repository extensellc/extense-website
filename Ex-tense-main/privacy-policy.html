Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

1. Overview

Extense LLC ("Extense," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage our services. By using our Site, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact Forms: Name, email address, company name, phone number, and message content.
  • Career Applications: Resume, cover letter, portfolio links, and work history.
  • Client Engagements: Business contact information and project-related communications.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, and timestamps.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand usage patterns. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide requested services.
  • To process career applications.
  • To send project updates and invoices to existing clients.
  • To improve our website and service offerings through analytics.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Hosting (AWS), email delivery, and CRM tools that process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption for data in transit, encrypted storage for data at rest, role-based access controls, and regular security audits. For clients in regulated industries (defense, healthcare), we provide additional security controls as specified in the SOW, including ITAR-compliant workflows and HIPAA Business Associate Agreements.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected. Contact form submissions are retained for 2 years. Client project data is retained for 5 years after engagement completion unless contractually specified otherwise.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request data portability.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (GDPR, CCPA).

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@ex-tense.co.
